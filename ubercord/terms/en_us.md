## 1. What we do here

The Übercord Provisional Service provides provisional Discord accounts to users who own the game Minecraft, developed by Mojang Studios. The service does this by interacting with servers affiliated with Mojang in order to attempt to prove that you own the game.

The Provisional Service additionally provides some helpers to create, delete, add users to, and remove users from lobbies.
